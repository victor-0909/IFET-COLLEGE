About Us

IFET college of Engineering is an Autonomous college of Engineering, Technology and Management located in Valavanur, Villupuram, Tamil Nadu, India. Known for its predominant ranking by the university and placements in the rural region of Tamil Nadu. It was established in 1998 by the Indo French Educational Trust.
